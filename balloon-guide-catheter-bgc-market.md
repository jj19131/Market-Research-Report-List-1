<p><p>A Balloon Guide Catheter (BGC) is a specialized medical device used in interventional procedures such as neurovascular interventions. It is designed to facilitate the passage of other devices, such as stent retrievers and aspiration catheters, to the target site within the blood vessels of the brain. The balloon portion of the catheter can be inflated to temporarily block blood flow, creating a stable platform for the delivery of therapeutic devices.</p><p>The Balloon Guide Catheter (BGC) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12% during the forecast period. <p><p>Balloon Guide Catheter (BGC) market consists of different types based on the size of the catheter: 7 F, 8 F, 9 F, and other sizes. The "F" refers to French units, which measure the diameter of the catheter. A 7 F catheter has a smaller diameter compared to an 8 F or 9 F catheter. Each size of BGC offers unique benefits in terms of flexibility, navigability, and aspiration capabilities. Healthcare providers choose the appropriate size based on the specific clinical scenario and patient anatomy.</p></p>
