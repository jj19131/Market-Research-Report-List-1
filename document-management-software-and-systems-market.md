<p><p>Document management software and systems are tools used by organizations to store, organize, and manage electronic documents. The market is divided into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve installing the software on a company's own servers, providing greater control and security but requiring more maintenance. Cloud-based solutions, on the other hand, store documents on remote servers, accessible through the internet. These offer convenience, scalability, and flexibility, with lower upfront costs. Organizations must consider their specific needs, resources, and priorities when choosing between on-premise and cloud-based document management systems.</p></p>
