<p><p>Polyurethane elastic sealants are available in two main types - one-component and two-component. One-component polyurethane elastic sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, making them convenient for quick applications. On the other hand, two-component polyurethane elastic sealants require mixing of two components before application, offering a stronger and more durable seal. Both types of sealants provide excellent adhesion, flexibility, and weather resistance, making them ideal for sealing joints and gaps in various construction applications.</p></p>
