<p><p>A Double Beam Spectrophotometer is an analytical instrument used to measure the absorption of light by a sample. It works by splitting the light source into two beams, one passing through the sample and the other through a reference material. By comparing the intensities of the two beams, the spectrophotometer can accurately determine the concentration of the analyte in the sample.</p><p>The Double Beam Spectrophotometer Market is witnessing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 9.1% during the forecast period.
