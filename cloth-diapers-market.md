<p><strong>The Cloth Diapers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pocket</li><li>Fitted</li><li>All-in-ones</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloth diapers market consists of three main types: pocket, fitted, and all-in-ones. Pocket diapers have a pocket opening where absorbent inserts can be placed, providing customizable absorbency. Fitted diapers are contoured to fit around the baby and require a separate waterproof cover. All-in-ones are convenient as they have the absorbent layer sewn into the diaper and do not require any additional pieces. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of parents looking for eco-friendly and cost-effective diapering options.</p></p>
