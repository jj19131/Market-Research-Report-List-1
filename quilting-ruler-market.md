These rulers are designed to prevent slippage on fabric, resulting in more accurate cuts and reducing the risk of injuries. Additionally, there is a growing demand for quilting rulers with specialty shapes, such as circles, curves, and hexagons, to create unique and intricate quilt designs.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a shift towards eco-friendly and sustainable quilting rulers.
