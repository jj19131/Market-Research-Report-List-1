<p><p>Small animal imaging market is segmented into three types: micro-MRI, optical, and nuclear imaging. Micro-MRI enables high-resolution imaging of small animals with detailed anatomical information. Optical imaging utilizes light signals to visualize biological processes in small animals, providing functional and molecular information. Nuclear imaging involves the administration of radioactive tracers to small animals, allowing the visualization of physiological processes. These imaging technologies aid in understanding disease mechanisms, preclinical drug development, and facilitating translational research, ultimately helping researchers and clinicians make more informed decisions.</p></p>
