<p><p>Telehealth platforms can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based telehealth platforms operate on the internet, allowing healthcare providers to access and manage patient information remotely. These platforms offer flexibility and scalability, as they can be easily accessed from any location or device. On the other hand, on-premises telehealth platforms are installed and operated locally within healthcare facilities. This type of platform provides more control over data and security but may require substantial upfront investment for hardware and infrastructure.</p></p>
