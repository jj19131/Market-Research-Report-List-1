<p><p>The market for aluminum composite panels can be categorized into four types: common panels, anti-fire panels, anti-bacteria panels, and antistatic panels. Common panels are widely used for various applications due to their durability and cost-effectiveness. Anti-fire panels are designed to resist fire and are commonly used in buildings where fire safety is a priority. Anti-bacteria panels are specifically designed to inhibit the growth of bacteria, making them ideal for hospitals and healthcare facilities. Antistatic panels are used in environments that require protection against static electricity, such as laboratories and electronics manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
