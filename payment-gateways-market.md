<p><p>Online mode payment gateways refer to the use of digital platforms to process transactions over the internet. These gateways facilitate real-time authorization and secure transmission of payment details from customers to merchants, ensuring a seamless online shopping experience. On the other hand, offline mode payment gateways involve physical terminals or POS systems that enable in-person transactions, typically using debit or credit cards. These gateways are commonly found in brick-and-mortar stores, allowing customers to make payments conveniently by swiping or inserting their cards at the point of sale.</p></p>
