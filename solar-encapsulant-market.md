<p><p>Solar encapsulants are used in photovoltaic modules to protect the solar cells from external factors and to ensure durability. The main types of solar encapsulants are Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA), Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB), Polyolefin Elastomer, and Others. EVA is the most commonly used type due to its cost-effectiveness and reliable performance. PVB offers higher transparency and adhesion properties. Polyolefin Elastomers are known for their excellent mechanical properties. Other types include silicone-based encapsulants, fluoropolymers, and thermoplastic resins.</p></p>
