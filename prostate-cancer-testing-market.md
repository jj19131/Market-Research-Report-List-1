<p><p>The Prostate Cancer Testing Market includes various types of tests such as PSA tests, CTC tests, Immunohistochemistry, PCA3 tests, and others. PSA tests measure the level of prostate-specific antigen in blood. CTC tests detect circulating tumor cells to determine the presence of cancer. Immunohistochemistry uses antibodies to detect specific proteins in prostate tissue samples. PCA3 tests measure the level of a specific gene associated with prostate cancer. Other tests may include imaging techniques or genetic testing. These tests aid in diagnosing and monitoring prostate cancer.</p></p>
