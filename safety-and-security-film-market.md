<p><p>Safety and security films are used to protect buildings and vehicles from break-ins, accidents, and natural disasters. Interior films are applied to windows and glass doors to prevent shattering and hold glass pieces together in case of impact. Exterior films are used on the outer surface of windows to provide additional protection against forced entry and extreme weather conditions. Both types of films enhance the safety and security of occupants and assets by reinforcing glass structures and reducing the risk of injuries and property damage.</p></p>
