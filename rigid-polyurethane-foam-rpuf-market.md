Molded RPUF is molded into specific shapes and sizes for various applications like packaging and automotive parts. Spray RPUF is applied using a spray gun for insulation in buildings. Other types of RPUF include specialty foams used in industries like aerospace and medical. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
