<p><strong>The Endoscope Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Hole Mirror Robot</li><li>Single-Hole Mirror Robot</li><li>No-Hole Mirror Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endoscope robot market is divided into three types based on the design of the mirror used for imaging inside the body. Multi-hole mirror robots have multiple holes on the mirror surface for better image quality and flexibility. Single-hole mirror robots have one hole for a simpler design and less complexity. No-hole mirror robots use advanced imaging technology to capture images without the need for a physical hole in the mirror. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
