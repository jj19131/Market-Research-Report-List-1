<p><p>Hydroxypropyl starch ether (HPSE) is a white or off-white powder that is derived from starch through modification using propylene oxide. In the construction industry, it is primarily used as an additive in cement-based materials and mortar to improve workability and enhance the performance of building materials. Growing construction activities worldwide are driving the demand for HPSE.</p><p>In the pharmaceutical industry, hydroxypropyl starch ether is used as a binder, thickener, and disintegrating agent in tablet formulations. The rising demand for pharmaceutical products, coupled with the increasing focus on drug delivery systems, is expected to drive the market growth.</p><p>In the cosmetics industry, HPSE is used in various personal care products such as creams, lotions, and gels as a thickening agent and stabilizer.
