<p><p>LVDT position sensors can be classified into DC and AC market types based on their operation and output signals. DC LVDT sensors provide a linear output voltage proportional to the position of the core, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy and stability. On the other hand, AC LVDT sensors use alternating current excitation to measure the linear displacement of the core, making them suitable for applications that require high sensitivity and precision in dynamic environments. Both types have specific advantages and are used in various industries.</p></p>
