<p><p>Accelerator Pedal Modules (APM) are divided into two main types in the market: Floor Type and Suspended Type. Floor Type APMs are attached directly to the vehicle floor and require a mechanical link to the engine. On the other hand, Suspended Type APMs are suspended from the dashboard and use an electronic linkage to control the engine. Both types of APMs serve the same purpose of controlling the acceleration of the vehicle, but they differ in their design and installation method.</p></p>
