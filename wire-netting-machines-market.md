One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for wire nets in the construction and agriculture sectors. Additionally, the growing awareness about the benefits of using wire nets in various industries, such as crop protection in agriculture, has further contributed to market growth.</p><p>Technological advancements in wire netting machines have also facilitated market growth.
