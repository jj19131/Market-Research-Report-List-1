<p><p>Electric Bus & Coach Market Types include Full Battery Electric Buses (BEB), Hybrid Electric Buses (HEB), and Fuel Cell Electric Buses (FCEB). BEBs run solely on electric power stored in rechargeable batteries, offering zero emissions. HEBs combine electric motor with an internal combustion engine, utilizing both electricity and fuel for operation. FCEBs generate electricity through a chemical reaction between hydrogen and oxygen, emitting only water vapor as a byproduct. Each type of electric bus technology offers unique advantages in terms of environmental sustainability and energy efficiency.</p></p>
