<p><p>The waterproof sheet material market offers three main types: Polyester filler (PY), Glass fiber filler (G), and Glass fiber reinforced polyester filler (PYG). PY sheets are made with polyester fillers, providing good insulation properties. G sheets use glass fiber fillers, making them strong and durable. PYG sheets combine both polyester and glass fiber fillers, resulting in a material that is both durable and offers good insulation properties. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the waterproof sheet material market.</p></p>
