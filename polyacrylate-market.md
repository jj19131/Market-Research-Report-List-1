<p><p>Polyacrylate market types include Poly(Ethyl Acrylate), Poly(Methyl Acrylate), Poly(Butyl Acrylate), and Others. These types are differentiated based on the different alkyl groups attached to the acrylate monomer. Poly(Ethyl Acrylate) is derived from ethyl acrylate monomer, Poly(Methyl Acrylate) from methyl acrylate monomer, and Poly(Butyl Acrylate) from butyl acrylate monomer. The "Others" category includes various other polyacrylates with different alkyl groups. Each type has unique properties and applications in industries such as coatings, adhesives, and textiles.</p></p>
