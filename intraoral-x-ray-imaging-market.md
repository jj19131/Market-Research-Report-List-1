<p><strong>The Intraoral X-ray Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bitewing X-rays</li><li>Periapical X-rays</li><li>Occlusal X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intraoral X-ray imaging is a type of dental imaging that allows dentists to capture detailed images of the teeth and surrounding structures. There are three main types of intraoral X-rays: Bitewing X-rays, which show the upper and lower teeth in one area of the mouth, Periapical X-rays, which focus on a specific tooth and its root, and Occlusal X-rays, which show the roof or floor of the mouth. These different types of X-rays help dentists diagnose issues such as cavities, gum disease, and tooth fractures.</p></p>
