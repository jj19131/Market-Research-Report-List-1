<p><p>Low aromatic solvents, also known as low-scent solvents, are organic solvents with low levels of aromatics, such as benzene, toluene, and xylene. These solvents are widely used in various industries, including paints and coatings, adhesives, printing inks, cleaners, and degreasers, due to their excellent solvency properties.</p><p>The market for low aromatic solvents is witnessing significant growth due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing demand for these solvents in the paints and coatings industry, primarily driven by the expanding construction industry, is boosting market growth. These solvents are extensively used in paint formulations as they provide enhanced film formation and better gloss, along with reduced environmental and health risks.</p><p>Additionally, the rising environmental concerns and strict regulations against the use of highly aromatic solvents are driving the market demand for low aromatic alternatives. Low aromatic solvents are considered safer and less toxic compared to their counterparts, making them the preferred choice in various end-use applications.</p><p>Moreover, the growing automotive industry, particularly in emerging economies, is fueling market growth. Low aromatic solvents are used in the production of adhesives, sealants, and coatings for automotive applications, including corrosion protection, surface finishing, and refinishing.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing various technological advancements and product innovations to meet the evolving consumer demands and environmental regulations. Manufacturers are focusing on developing low aromatic solvents with improved characteristics, such as higher flash points, lower toxicity, and reduced emissions.</p><p>In conclusion, the low aromatic solvents market is projected to experience a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.8% during the forecast period. Factors such as increasing demand from the paints and coatings industry, stringent regulations, and technological advancements are driving the market growth.</p></p>
