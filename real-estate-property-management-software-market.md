<p><p>Real estate property management software can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, allowing users to store data and access the software from any location with an internet connection. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed on local servers and accessed through an organization's internal network, requiring physical infrastructure and IT support.
