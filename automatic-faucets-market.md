<p><p>Automatic faucets can be categorized into two main types based on their power source: DC and AC market. DC automatic faucets are powered by direct current, typically using batteries or low-voltage power sources. On the other hand, AC automatic faucets are powered by alternating current, typically using a standard electrical outlet. Both types offer convenient hands-free operation, water conservation benefits, and hygienic advantages. The choice between DC and AC faucets depends on factors such as installation location, power availability, and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
