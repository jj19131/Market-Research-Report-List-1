<p><p>End User Computing (EUC) refers to the systems and technologies that enable non-technical end users to access and use computer applications and services without the need for assistance from IT professionals. These technologies are revolutionizing the way end users interact with applications and data, offering seamless access and improved user experience. Additionally, the adoption of Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) policies in many organizations is contributing to the market growth, as employees prefer using their personal devices for work purposes.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising popularity of workspace virtualization solutions is driving market growth.
