<p><p>The Automotive Engine Cooling Module Market consists of various types, with the main ones being Radiator, Condenser and Fan System Module and Radiator and Condenser Module. The Radiator, Condenser, and Fan System Module includes all three components working together to cool the engine. On the other hand, the Radiator and Condenser Module includes just the radiator and condenser, which are responsible for dissipating heat from the engine coolant. Both types are crucial in ensuring proper engine cooling and preventing overheating.</p></p>
