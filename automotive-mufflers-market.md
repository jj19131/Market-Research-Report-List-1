<p><p>Automotive mufflers are a critical component of a vehicle's exhaust system, designed to reduce noise levels generated by the engine. There are two main types of mufflers in the market: Absorptive Mufflers and Reactive Mufflers. Absorptive mufflers use sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise levels, while reactive mufflers use chambers and baffles to cancel out sound waves. Both types are essential for ensuring a quieter and more comfortable driving experience, with each offering unique advantages in terms of noise reduction and performance.</p></p>
