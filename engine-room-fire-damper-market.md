<p><p>Engine room fire dampers are classified into four main types: AC dampers, HT dampers, AL dampers, and SF dampers. AC dampers are designed for use in air conditioning systems, HT dampers are for high-temperature environments, AL dampers are for use in aluminum ducts, and SF dampers have smoke control capabilities. Each type of damper is specialized for different applications, providing crucial fire protection and ventilation control in engine rooms and other industrial settings.</p></p>
