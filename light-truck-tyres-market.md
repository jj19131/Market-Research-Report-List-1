<p><strong>The Light Truck Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tyres</li><li>Bias Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light truck tyres are categorized into two main types: radial tyres and bias tyres. Radial tyres have cords that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and durability. On the other hand, bias tyres have cords that run at an angle across the tyre, providing more stability and load-carrying capacity. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different applications, depending on factors such as road conditions, load capacity, and driving preferences.</p></p>
