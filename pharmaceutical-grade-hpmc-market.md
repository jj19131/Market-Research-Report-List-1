<p><p>Pharmaceutical Grade HPMC is categorized into three main types based on their viscosity levels - Low Viscosity HPMC, Middle Viscosity HPMC, and High Viscosity HPMC. Low Viscosity HPMC is used for immediate-release tablets, while Middle Viscosity HPMC is suitable for sustained-release tablets. High Viscosity HPMC is commonly used in controlled-release formulations. Each type of HPMC offers unique benefits and characteristics, allowing pharmaceutical companies to tailor their formulations to achieve specific release profiles and drug delivery requirements.</p></p>
