<p><ul><li>Single Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li><li>Dual-Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li><li>Multi-Zone Rotary Tube Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary tube furnaces are available in various market types, including Single Zone, Dual-Zone, and Multi-Zone configurations. Single Zone furnaces have a single heating zone along the length of the tube, providing uniform heat treatment. Dual-Zone furnaces have two independent heating zones, allowing for different temperature profiles within the same tube. Multi-Zone furnaces have three or more heating zones, offering even more precise temperature control and versatility. Each type of furnace caters to different heat treatment requirements and processes in various industries.</p></p>
