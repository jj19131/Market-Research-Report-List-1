<p><p>Cold seal adhesives are used for packaging applications where the adhesive is activated by pressure and does not require heat to bond. There are three main types of cold seal adhesives based on the solvents used: water-based cold seal adhesives, solvent-based cold seal adhesives, and solvent-less cold seal adhesives. Water-based cold seal adhesives are environmentally friendly and easy to use. Solvent-based cold seal adhesives offer strong bonding properties. Solvent-less cold seal adhesives are free from harmful solvents, making them safe for use in food packaging applications.</p></p>
