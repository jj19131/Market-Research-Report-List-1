<p><p>RO Membrane Antiscalants are used to prevent scaling on the membrane surface in water treatment systems. There are two main types of RO Membrane Antiscalants - Acid Antiscalants and Alkaline Antiscalants. Acid Antiscalants are used to prevent scaling in low pH waters, while Alkaline Antiscalants are used in high pH waters. Both types work by inhibiting the formation of scale deposits on the membrane surface, ensuring the efficient operation of the RO system.</p></p>
