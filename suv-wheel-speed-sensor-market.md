<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SUV Wheel Speed Sensor market encompasses two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall-effect transducer to detect the speed of the wheel, while Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize magnets and electric signals to measure wheel speed. Both types are crucial components in modern SUVs, providing essential data for the vehicle's ABS and traction control systems. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
