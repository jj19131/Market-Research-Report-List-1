<p><ul><li>Iohexol</li><li>Iodixanol</li><li>Iopamidol</li><li>Ioversol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CT contrast media are used to enhance the visibility of internal body structures during imaging procedures. Common types include Iohexol, Iodixanol, Iopamidol, and Ioversol. Iohexol and Iodixanol are non-ionic contrast agents with low osmolality, reducing the risk of adverse reactions. Iopamidol is an ionic contrast agent with high osmolality, suitable for patients with normal kidney function. Ioversol is another non-ionic contrast agent with low osmolality, often preferred for its safety profile. Each type offers unique benefits for different patient populations and imaging needs.</p></p>
