<p><p>Cemented carbide market types consist of tungsten carbide (WC), titanium carbide (TiC), and tantalum carbide (TaC). Tungsten carbide is known for its hardness and wear resistance, making it suitable for cutting tools and drilling equipment. Titanium carbide offers high temperature resistance and is commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries. Tantalum carbide is valued for its toughness and is utilized in high-temperature applications such as machining and metal cutting. These carbides play a crucial role in various industries due to their unique properties.</p></p>
