<p><p>Smart windows materials market is classified into three main types based on their mechanism of action. Photochromic materials change their optical properties in response to sunlight, allowing windows to become darker in bright conditions. Electrochromic materials use an electric current to change the tint of windows, providing instant control over light transmission. Thermochromic materials respond to temperature changes, offering a passive way to regulate sunlight entering a room. Each type of smart window material has its unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
