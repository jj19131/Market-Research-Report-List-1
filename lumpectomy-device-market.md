<p><p>A lumpectomy device is a surgical instrument used in a lumpectomy procedure, which is a type of breast-conserving surgery for treating breast cancer. The lumpectomy device helps in accurately locating and guiding the removal of the tumor.</p><p>The lumpectomy device market has been experiencing steady growth due to the increasing prevalence of breast cancer worldwide. Rising awareness about breast cancer screening programs and early detection, along with the growing preference for minimally invasive surgical procedures, are driving market growth.
