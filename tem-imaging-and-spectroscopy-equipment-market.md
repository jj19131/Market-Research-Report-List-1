<p><p>TEM imaging and spectroscopy equipment market types include EELS (Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy), EFTEM (Energy-filtered Transmission Electron Microscopy), and Others. EELS is a technique used to analyze the energy loss of electrons passing through a specimen, providing information on the elemental composition and chemical bonding. EFTEM uses an energy filter to selectively image specific elements or chemical groups within a specimen. Other market types may include various advanced TEM techniques and accessories for specialized applications in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
