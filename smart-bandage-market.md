<p><p>The smart bandage market includes two emerging types: graphene and synthetic fiber bandages. Graphene is a one-atom-thick carbon material with excellent electrical conductivity and flexibility, making it ideal for monitoring wound healing through sensors embedded in the bandage. Synthetic fiber bandages, on the other hand, are made from man-made materials such as polyester or nylon and offer properties like high tensile strength and durability. These bandages can incorporate smart features like temperature and moisture sensors to detect potential infections and ensure optimal healing conditions.</p></p>
