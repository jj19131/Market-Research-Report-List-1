<p><p>Dry grinding systems are used for processing materials without the use of liquids. The types of systems include fluidized bed jet mills, which use high-speed jets of compressed air or inert gas to grind materials; graded mills, which use screens or classifiers to control particle size distribution; fine cutting mills, which use sharp blades to cut materials into fine particles; and others which may include various types of equipment for dry grinding applications. Each type of system offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, particle size control, and energy consumption.</p></p>
