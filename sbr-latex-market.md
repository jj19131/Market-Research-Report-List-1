<p><ul><li>Emulsion SBR Latex</li><li>Solution SBR Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SBR Latex market can be divided into two types: Emulsion SBR Latex and Solution SBR Latex. Emulsion SBR Latex is an aqueous dispersion of SBR polymer particles, commonly used in industries like construction, textiles, and paper. On the other hand, Solution SBR Latex is a water-based solution of SBR polymer, often used in applications like adhesives, coatings, and sealants. Both types offer excellent bonding properties and resistance to abrasion, making them versatile options for various industries.</p></p>
