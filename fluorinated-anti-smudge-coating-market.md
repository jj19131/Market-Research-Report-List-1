<p><p>Fluorinated anti-smudge coatings are commonly categorized into two main types: silicon-based and silicon-free. Silicon-based coatings contain silicon compounds that provide excellent durability and high resistance to smudges and stains. On the other hand, silicon-free coatings are formulated without silicon compounds, making them eco-friendly and suitable for applications where silicon is not desired. Both types offer superior anti-smudge properties, but silicon-free coatings are preferred for their environmentally-friendly nature and versatility in various industries.</p></p>
