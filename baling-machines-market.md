<p><p>Baling machines are classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic baling machines operate with minimal human intervention, utilizing sensors and programmed settings to perform the baling process independently. On the other hand, semi-automatic baling machines require some manual input from operators, such as feeding materials and clearing jams, but still streamline the baling process compared to manual alternatives. Both types of machines offer efficiency and convenience in managing waste materials and increasing productivity in various industrial settings.</p></p>
