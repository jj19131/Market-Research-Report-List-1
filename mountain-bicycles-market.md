<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Hardtail</li><li>Softail</li><li>Full Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mountain bicycles are available in four main market types- rigid, hardtail, softail, and full suspension. Rigid mountain bikes have no suspension and are lightweight, making them ideal for smooth trails. Hardtail bikes have a front suspension fork, providing some shock absorption on rough terrains. Softail bikes feature a rear suspension design that absorbs impact from the rear wheel. Full suspension bikes have both front and rear suspension, offering the highest level of shock absorption and comfort on the most challenging trails.</p></p>
