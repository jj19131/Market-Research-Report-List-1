<p><p>Linear DC servomotors are classified based on their voltage requirements into three types: low voltage, mid voltage, and high voltage markets. Low voltage linear DC servomotors typically operate in the range of 0-100V, making them suitable for small-scale applications. Mid voltage servomotors operate in the range of 100-600V, ideal for medium-sized industrial applications. High voltage servomotors are designed to operate at voltages exceeding 600V, making them suitable for large-scale industrial systems where high power output and precision control are required.</p></p>
