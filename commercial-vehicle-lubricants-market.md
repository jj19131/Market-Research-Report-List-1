<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Lubricants Market mainly comprises of three major types - Engine Oil, Transmission Fluids, and Brake Fluids. Engine oil is used to lubricate internal combustion engines, reducing friction and wear on moving parts. Transmission fluids are essential for cooling and lubricating transmission components for smooth gear shifting. Brake fluids are used for hydraulic brake systems, transmitting force to the brake pads for stopping the vehicle. These lubricants play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and longevity of commercial vehicles.</p></p>
