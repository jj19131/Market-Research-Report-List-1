<p><p>Truck and Bus Tires market consists of two main types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias Tires are constructed with multiple fabric layers crisscrossing each other at a specific angle, providing durability and strength. On the other hand, Radial Tires have fabric layers arranged at a 90-degree angle from the tread centerline, offering better fuel efficiency and longer tread life. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the commercial vehicle industry.</p></p>
