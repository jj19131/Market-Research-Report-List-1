<p><p>Anionic surfactants are categorized into different types based on their chemical structure and properties. Alpha Olefin Sulfonates (AOS) are used in industrial applications due to their high cleaning ability. Secondary Alkane Sulfonate (SAS) is used in agricultural formulations. Methyl Ester Sulfonates (MES) are popular in laundry detergents. Sulfosuccinates are widely used in personal care products and other specialty applications. Other anionic surfactants cater to niche markets with specific needs.</p></p>
