<p><p>Roll laminators come in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual roll laminators require the user to manually feed the documents through the machine and adjust settings as needed. These are typically more affordable and better suited for smaller-volume laminating needs. On the other hand, automatic roll laminators are designed to feed documents through the machine automatically, making them more efficient for larger-scale laminating projects. They are often equipped with advanced features and are ideal for high-volume usage.</p></p>
