<p><p>Oil & Gas Plunger Lifts mainly come in two types - Single Pad Plunger Type and Dual Pad Plunger Type. Single Pad Plunger Type is designed with one plunger that moves up and down within the well tubing to lift accumulated liquids and gas to the surface. On the other hand, Dual Pad Plunger Type consists of two plungers, which allow for more efficient liquid removal and gas lift operations. Both types are commonly used in the oil and gas industry for enhancing production and improving well performance.</p></p>
