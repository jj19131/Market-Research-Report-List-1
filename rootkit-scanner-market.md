<p><p>Rootkit scanner market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based rootkit scanners operate on remote servers and do not require any installation or maintenance on the user's end. They offer scalability, accessibility, and instant updates, making them suitable for businesses with limited IT resources. On the other hand, on-premise rootkit scanners are installed directly on the user's systems. They provide more control over data security and are commonly preferred by organizations with strict compliance and regulatory requirements. Both types serve the purpose of detecting and removing malicious rootkits but differ in deployment and management approaches.</p></p>
