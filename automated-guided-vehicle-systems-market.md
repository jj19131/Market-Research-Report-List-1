<p><p>Automated Guided Vehicle Systems (AGVS) are widely classified into three types: Magnetic Guided, Laser Guided, and Other Market. Magnetic guided AGVs follow magnetic strips embedded in the floor, providing precise navigation and flexibility for route changes. Laser guided AGVs use lasers for navigation and tend to be more expensive but offer high accuracy and adaptability to changing environments. The Other Market category includes AGVs that use technologies such as vision-based navigation or inertial guidance systems, offering unique solutions for specific industry needs.</p></p>
