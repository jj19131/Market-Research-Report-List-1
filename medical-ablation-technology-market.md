One prominent trend is the increasing adoption of radiofrequency ablation (RFA) and microwave ablation (MWA) technologies. <p><p>Medical ablation technology refers to the use of various methods to destroy or remove abnormal tissue in the body. There are several types of ablation techniques used in the market. Thermal ablation uses heat to destroy tissue, whereas radiofrequency ablation utilizes high-frequency electrical currents. Microwave ablation employs electromagnetic energy, laser ablation uses focused light energy, and cryoablation involves freezing the tissue to destroy it. These diverse techniques offer options for physicians to treat different conditions based on the specific needs of patients.</p></p>
