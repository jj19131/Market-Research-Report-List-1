<p><p>Insulation coating market offers a variety of types such as acrylics, polyurethane, epoxy, YSZ, and Mullite. Acrylic coatings are water-based and provide good durability. Polyurethane coatings offer high flexibility and resistance to heat. Epoxy coatings are known for their chemical resistance and adhesion properties.
