<p><p>Surface PMSM (SPMSM) Type market involves motors where permanent magnets are located on the rotor surface, resulting in a compact design and efficient operation. On the other hand, Interior PMSM (IPMSM) Type market consists of motors with permanent magnets embedded within the rotor, offering better heat dissipation and higher torque density. Both types are widely used in automotive applications for their high efficiency, reliability, and smooth operation, making them popular choices for electric vehicle propulsion systems and other automotive applications.</p></p>
