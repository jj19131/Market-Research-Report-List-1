<p><ul><li>On-premise</li><li>Software-as-a-service (SaaS)</li><li>Cloud-hosted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Payroll Management System market comprises three main types: On-premise, Software-as-a-service (SaaS), and Cloud-hosted. On-premise systems are installed and operated on the user's local servers, providing complete control and customization but requiring maintenance and infrastructure costs. SaaS refers to software accessed via the internet, where the provider hosts and maintains the system. It offers scalability, automatic updates, and lower upfront costs. Cloud-hosted systems are similar to SaaS but utilize remote servers, providing flexibility, accessibility, and seamless integration, with data storage and processing taking place in the cloud infrastructure.</p></p>
