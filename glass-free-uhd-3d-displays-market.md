<p><p>Glass-free UHD 3D displays come in two main types, Parallax Barrier Technology and Lenticular Display Technology. Parallax Barrier Technology uses a series of slits placed over the display to create a 3D effect by directing light to each eye separately. Lenticular Display Technology, on the other hand, uses an array of cylindrical lenses that creates a different image for each eye, resulting in a 3D effect without the need for glasses. Both technologies aim to provide a glasses-free 3D viewing experience for consumers.</p></p>
