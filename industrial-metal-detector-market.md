<p><strong>The Industrial Metal Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rectangular Aperture Metal Detectors</li><li>Gravity Fall Metal Detectors</li><li>Pipeline Liquid, Paste and Slurry Metal Detectors</li><li>Metal Detectors With Conveyor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial metal detector market can be categorized into various types. Rectangular aperture metal detectors are used to detect metal contaminants in products conveyed through a production line. Gravity fall metal detectors identify metal impurities in free-flowing products. Pipeline liquid, paste, and slurry metal detectors are used to inspect liquid-based products in pipes. Metal detectors with conveyor systems are employed to scan products on a conveyor belt for the presence of metal contaminants. These different types cater to specific industries and applications while ensuring product safety and quality.</p></p>
