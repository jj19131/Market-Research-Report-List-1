<p><p>Neurovascular intervention devices are used in the treatment of various neurological conditions. Cerebral angioplasty and stenting systems involve the use of balloons and stents to open blocked blood vessels in the brain. Aneurysm coiling and embolization devices are used to treat cerebral aneurysms by inserting coils or other embolic agents to stop blood flow. Support devices assist in preventing blood clot formation or providing mechanical support to the blood vessels. Neuro thrombectomy devices are utilized to remove blood clots from the brain in cases of acute ischemic stroke.</p></p>
