<p><ul><li>Qualitative Filtration Papers</li><li>Quantitative Filtration Papers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtration papers are categorized into two main types: Qualitative Filtration Papers and Quantitative Filtration Papers. Qualitative papers are used for general filtration applications where the presence or absence of a particular substance is being determined. They are commonly used in educational and research laboratories. On the other hand, Quantitative Filtration Papers are designed for precise measurement and quantification of substances in a sample. They are used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing for accurate analysis and quality control.</p></p>
