<p><strong>The Automotive Height Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive height sensors can be classified into two types: analog and digital. Analog height sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies in voltage or current depending on the height of the vehicle. On the other hand, digital height sensors give a discrete output signal in the form of binary code or digital data to indicate the vehicle's height. Both types of sensors are used in automotive suspension systems to monitor and adjust the vehicle's height for better performance and comfort.</p></p>
