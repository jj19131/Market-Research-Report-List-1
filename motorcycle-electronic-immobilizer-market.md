<p><p>Built-in type immobilizers are integrated into the motorcycle's electronic system, making it more discreet and difficult for thieves to tamper with. External type immobilizers are separate devices that can be added to the motorcycle for an extra layer of security. Built-in type immobilizers are often preferred for their convenience and seamless integration, while external type immobilizers are popular for their flexibility and ease of installation on older or aftermarket motorcycles. Both types help prevent unauthorized use of the motorcycle by immobilizing the engine.</p></p>
