<p><ul><li>A-mode</li><li>B-mode or 2D mode</li><li>C-mode</li><li>M-mode</li><li>Doppler mode</li><li>Pulse inversion mode</li><li>Harmonic mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable ultrasound machines come in various market types to cater to different diagnostic needs. A-mode provides information on tissue depth, B-mode or 2D mode shows images of structures in the body. C-mode is used for cardiac imaging, M-mode for studying movement, Doppler mode for evaluating blood flow, Pulse inversion mode for better image clarity, and Harmonic mode for improved resolution. Each market type serves a specific purpose in diagnosing various medical conditions, making portable ultrasound machines versatile and essential in healthcare settings.</p></p>
