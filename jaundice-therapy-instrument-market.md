<p><p>The Jaundice Therapy Instrument market consists of two main types: Conventional Phototherapy and Fiberoptic Phototherapy. Conventional Phototherapy utilizes special blue lights to help break down bilirubin in newborns' blood, which causes jaundice. It is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and simplicity. On the other hand, Fiberoptic Phototherapy uses fiber optic cables to deliver light directly to the baby's skin, focusing the treatment and reducing potential side effects. Although it is a relatively newer technology, it has gained popularity for its improved effectiveness and reduced risks.</p></p>
