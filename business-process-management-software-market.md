<p><p>Business Process Management (BPM) software is categorized into two main market types: Cloud and On-Premise. Cloud-based BPM software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. It allows businesses to easily collaborate and access features from various locations. On the other hand, On-Premise BPM software is installed and hosted locally on the company's servers. It offers higher security and control but requires additional IT infrastructure and maintenance. Both types cater to different business needs, and selecting the right type depends on factors like budget, data security, and scalability requirements.</p></p>
