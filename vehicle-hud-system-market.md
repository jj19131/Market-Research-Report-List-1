<p><p>Vehicle HUD system market is segmented into three main types - WHUD, AR-HUD, and C-HUD. WHUD, also known as windshield HUD, projects information directly onto the windshield, allowing drivers to view data without taking their eyes off the road. AR-HUD, or augmented reality HUD, enhances the driving experience by overlaying information such as navigation and speed on the windshield. C-HUD, or combiner HUD, displays information on a small screen located on the dashboard, offering a more compact solution for drivers.</p></p>
