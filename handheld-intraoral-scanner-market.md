<p><p>A handheld intraoral scanner is a dental device used to create a digital impression of a patient's teeth and gums. The driving factors behind this growth include the increasing prevalence of dental diseases, the growing demand for cosmetic dentistry, and the advantages offered by handheld intraoral scanners over traditional dental impression methods. These scanners provide faster and more comfortable scanning experiences for patients, improve workflow efficiency for dentists, and allow for seamless integration with digital dentistry workflows.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of more compact and user-friendly handheld intraoral scanners, further boosting market growth. <p><p>The handheld intraoral scanner market can be categorized into two types: wired scanners and wireless scanners.
