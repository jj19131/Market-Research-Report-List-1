<p><p>Electrically Trikes market is divided into two main types based on motor placement: Side Wheel Motors and Center Motors. Side Wheel Motors are mounted on the rear wheels of the trike, providing power directly to the wheels for propulsion. Center Motors, on the other hand, are positioned in the middle of the trike, usually between the pedals, providing a more balanced and efficient power distribution. Both types offer distinct advantages and cater to different preferences and requirements of users in the electric trikes market.</p></p>
