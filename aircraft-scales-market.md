<p><p>Platform and floor-standing aircraft scales are two main types in the market. Platform scales are designed with a flat surface on which the aircraft can be placed for weighing. These scales are versatile and can accommodate a range of aircraft sizes. On the other hand, floor-standing scales are typically larger and sturdier, capable of weighing heavier aircraft. They are often used in larger airports and maintenance facilities. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate weight measurements for aircraft safety and efficiency.</p></p>
