<p><p>The background screening market encompasses two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based background screening refers to the use of online platforms to conduct screening processes, storing and analyzing data on external servers. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness due to the elimination of hardware and maintenance expenses. On the other hand, on-premise background screening involves conducting screenings using local resources and storing data on internal servers. This type provides more control over data and may be preferred by organizations with high-security requirements or regulatory constraints.</p></p>
