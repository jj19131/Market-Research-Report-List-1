<p><p>Marker pens can be categorized into two main types: oily (alcoholic) markers and water-based markers. Oily markers are known for their vibrant colors and quick-drying ink, making them ideal for use on surfaces like plastic, metal, and glass. On the other hand, water-based markers are great for paper-based projects as they offer a more subtle and blendable color payoff. Both types of marker pens cater to different needs and preferences, providing a wide range of options for artists, students, and professionals alike.</p></p>
