<p><p>Air pilot valves are commonly categorized into two types: single market and double market. Single market valves have only one exhaust port and are typically used in applications with simple pneumatic systems. Both types of valves play a crucial role in regulating the flow of air and controlling the operation of pneumatic devices in various industrial and manufacturing settings.</p></p>
