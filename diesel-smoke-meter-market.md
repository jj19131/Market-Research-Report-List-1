<p><p>A Diesel Smoke Meter is a device used to measure the opacity or density of smoke emitted from diesel engines. It is a crucial tool in checking the emissions from diesel vehicles and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. The meter works by passing a light beam through the exhaust gas and measuring the amount of light absorbed, with the results displayed as a percentage of smoke density.</p><p>The Diesel Smoke Meter Market is expected to experience significant growth, with a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
