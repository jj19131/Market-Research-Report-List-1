<p><p>Telephoto lenses are classified based on their magnification factor, which determines the extent of zoom capability. Under 1.4x lenses offer a slight zoom, suitable for everyday photography. Lenses in the 1.4-1.5x range provide a moderate zoom for more detailed shots. 1.5-1.7x lenses offer a higher level of magnification, ideal for capturing distant subjects. 1.7-2x lenses provide a significant zoom range for professional photographers. Lenses over 2x offer the highest level of magnification, enabling extreme close-ups of distant objects.</p></p>
