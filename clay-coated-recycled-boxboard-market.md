<p><p>Clay Coated Recycled Boxboard is a type of packaging material commonly used in the food and beverage industry. Single Clay Coated Boxboard has one layer of clay coating on one side, providing a smooth and glossy finish suitable for printing. Double Clay Coated Boxboard, on the other hand, has a layer of clay coating on both sides, offering enhanced durability and stiffness. Single Clay Coated Boxboard is typically used for products where only one side needs to be printed, while Double Clay Coated Boxboard is preferred for products requiring high-quality printing on both sides.</p></p>
