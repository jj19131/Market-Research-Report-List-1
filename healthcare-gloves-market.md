<p><p>Healthcare gloves are disposable gloves that are worn by healthcare professionals during medical examinations and procedures to prevent the spread of infections and protect both the healthcare workers and patients from exposure to harmful substances. These gloves are typically made from latex, nitrile, vinyl, or neoprene and are available in various sizes. They provide a barrier between the user's hands and potentially infectious material, reducing the risk of transmission of diseases.</p><p>The healthcare gloves market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing cases of infectious diseases, rising awareness about personal hygiene, and strict regulations regarding the use of gloves in healthcare settings. The market is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 13.1%.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the healthcare gloves market is the growing preference for nitrile gloves over latex gloves. Nitrile gloves offer superior puncture resistance, chemical resistance, and durability compared to latex gloves. With an increasing number of healthcare workers developing latex allergies, the demand for nitrile gloves has surged.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the introduction of powder-free gloves. Powdered gloves were traditionally used to facilitate easy wearing and removal. However, the powder can cause skin irritation and respiratory issues. As a result, the demand for powder-free gloves has increased, ensuring better safety and comfort for users.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in glove manufacturing technologies are leading to the production of gloves with improved tactile sensitivity and dexterity. These gloves allow healthcare professionals to perform delicate procedures with better precision and accuracy, enhancing patient care.</p><p>In conclusion, the healthcare gloves market is expected to witness substantial growth driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of infectious diseases, rising awareness about hygiene practices, and the development of advanced glove technologies. The market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period.</p></p>
