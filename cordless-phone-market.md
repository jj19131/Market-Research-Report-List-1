<p><strong>The Cordless Phone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>DECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cordless phones can be categorized into two main market types: analog and DECT. Analog cordless phones use a traditional analog signal to transmit voice data, offering a simple and cost-effective solution for basic communication needs. On the other hand, DECT (Digital Enhanced Cordless Telecommunications) cordless phones utilize a digital signal to provide clearer sound quality, longer range, and better security features. DECT phones are known for their interference-free communication and ability to support multiple handsets within the same system.</p></p>
