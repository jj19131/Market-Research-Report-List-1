<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Oil Package Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>D-type Package Boilers</li><li>A-type Package Boilers</li><li>O-type Package Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil package boilers are categorized into three main types: D-type, A-type, and O-type. D-type boilers have a single large steam drum at the top and separate water and steam drums, providing high steam capacity and high steam purity. A-type boilers have two smaller water drums and separate steam drums, offering flexibility in steam capacity. O-type boilers have a single water drum and separate steam drums, making them compact and suitable for smaller spaces. Each type offers unique advantages for various industrial applications.</p></p>
