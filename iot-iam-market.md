<p><strong>The IoT IAM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Private Deployment Model</li><li>Public Deployment Model</li><li>Hybrid Deployment Model</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IoT IAM (Internet of Things Identity and Access Management) encompasses three market types: private, public, and hybrid deployment models. A private deployment model refers to an IoT IAM system hosted and managed by an organization on its premises, providing exclusive control and security. A public deployment model involves an IoT IAM system hosted and managed by a third-party service provider, offering scalability and convenience but with less control. A hybrid deployment model combines elements of both private and public models, allowing organizations to store sensitive data on-premises while utilizing external services for scalability and flexibility.</p></p>
