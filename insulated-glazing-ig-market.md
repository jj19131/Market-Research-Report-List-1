<p><p>Insulated Glazing (IG) refers to a type of window consisting of two or more glass panes separated by a space filled with gas. The four main types of IG are Float Insulated Glass, which is made by floating molten glass on molten tin; Tempered Insulated Glass, which is heat-treated for increased strength; Coated Insulated Glass, which has a special coating for added performance; and LOW-E Insulated Glass, which has a low-emissivity coating to improve energy efficiency. Each type offers different benefits for insulation and energy efficiency.</p></p>
