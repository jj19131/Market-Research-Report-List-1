<p><p>Automotive knee airbag modules are designed to provide additional protection to passengers in the event of a frontal collision. There are two main types in the market: driver's side knee airbags and front passenger knee airbags. Driver's side knee airbags are located below the steering column and deploy to protect the driver's legs and knees. Front passenger knee airbags are located in the dashboard and deploy to protect the front passenger's legs and knees. Both types of knee airbags help to reduce the risk of injury during a crash.</p></p>
