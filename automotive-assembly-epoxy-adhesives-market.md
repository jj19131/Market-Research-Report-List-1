<p><p>Automotive assembly epoxy adhesives are available in two main types: liquid and paste. Liquid epoxy adhesives are easy to apply and are ideal for applications where a thin layer of adhesive is needed. Paste epoxy adhesives, on the other hand, are thicker in consistency and are often used for filling gaps and bonding rough or uneven surfaces. Both types offer strong and durable bonds, making them popular choices for automotive assembly applications due to their ability to withstand vibrations, impact, and harsh environmental conditions.</p></p>
