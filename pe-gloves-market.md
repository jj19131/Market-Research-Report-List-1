<p><strong>The PE Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE)</li><li>Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE)</li><li>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE)</li><li>Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PE Gloves market types are categorized based on the type of polyethylene material used in their production. Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) gloves are flexible and have good chemical resistance. Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) gloves provide a balance of strength and flexibility. High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) gloves are strong and durable. Ultra-high Molecular Weight Polyethylene (UHMWPE) gloves offer exceptional abrasion resistance and low friction, making them ideal for demanding applications. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
