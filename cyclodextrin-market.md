<p><p>Cyclodextrin market types include alpha-cyclodextrin, beta-cyclodextrin, gamma-cyclodextrin, and cyclodextrin derivatives. Alpha-cyclodextrin is the smallest and most soluble in water, beta-cyclodextrin is the most commonly used in industries, and gamma-cyclodextrin has the highest solubility of the three. Cyclodextrin derivatives are modified forms of cyclodextrins with enhanced properties for specific applications. These market types cater to various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics due to their ability to encapsulate and stabilize molecules.</p></p>
