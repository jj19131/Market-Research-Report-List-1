<p><p>Parking detectors are devices used to assist drivers in safely maneuvering into parking spots. The market for parking detectors can be divided into three main types: ultrasound, radar, and others. Ultrasound parking detectors use sound waves to detect the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, while radar parking detectors use radio waves for the same purpose. Other types of parking detectors may include cameras or sensors. Each type has its own advantages and may be preferred depending on the specific needs of the driver.</p></p>
