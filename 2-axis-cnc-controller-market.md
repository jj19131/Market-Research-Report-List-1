<p><strong>The 2-Axis CNC Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Loop CNC Controller</li><li>Closed-Loop CNC Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>2-Axis CNC Controller market can be divided into two main types: Open-Loop CNC Controller and Closed-Loop CNC Controller. Open-Loop controllers do not provide feedback on the actual position of the axes, relying solely on the input commands for movement. Closed-Loop controllers, on the other hand, continuously monitor the position of the axes and make adjustments to ensure accurate positioning. While Open-Loop controllers are more cost-effective, Closed-Loop controllers offer higher precision and accuracy, making them preferred for applications requiring high levels of precision.</p></p>
