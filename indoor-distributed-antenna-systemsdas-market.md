<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor Distributed Antenna Systems, or DAS, are used to improve wireless coverage and capacity in indoor spaces. Active DAS uses a central hub to distribute signals to antennas throughout a building, allowing for greater control and optimization of the system. Passive DAS, on the other hand, relies on passive components like coaxial cables to distribute signals, making it a simpler and more cost-effective solution for smaller spaces. Both types of DAS systems play a crucial role in enhancing indoor wireless connectivity.</p></p>
