<p><p>Miniature power supplies can be categorized into two main types: AC-DC and DC-DC. AC-DC power supplies convert alternating current (AC) from the mains power source into direct current (DC) to power electronic devices. On the other hand, DC-DC power supplies convert one DC voltage level to another, making them ideal for applications where a different voltage is required. Both types of power supplies play a crucial role in various industries such as electronics, telecommunications, and medical devices.</p></p>
