<p><p>Foodservice gloves market can be categorized into three main types based on the material used: Latex Gloves, Rubber Gloves, and Non-Latex Gloves (Nitrile or Vinyl). Latex gloves are natural rubber gloves known for their elasticity and comfort. Rubber gloves are generally thicker and more durable, suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Non-latex gloves, such as nitrile or vinyl, are preferred by individuals with latex allergies. They offer similar protection and strength as latex gloves but without the risk of allergic reactions.</p></p>
