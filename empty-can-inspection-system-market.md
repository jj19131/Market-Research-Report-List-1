<p><p>Empty Can Inspection System Market Types include Optical Inspection, Ultrasonic Testing, X-ray Inspection, and Others. Optical Inspection involves using cameras to detect defects or abnormalities on the surface of the cans. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes sound waves to identify internal flaws in the cans. X-ray Inspection uses high-energy electromagnetic waves to penetrate the cans and identify any defects inside. Other types of inspection systems may include magnetic or eddy current testing methods, which can also be used to detect defects in empty cans.</p></p>
