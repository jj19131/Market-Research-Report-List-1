<p><p>Furniture casters market can be divided into two main types: Swivel casters and Rigid casters. Swivel casters have a 360-degree rotation capability, allowing for easy maneuverability and direction changes. They are commonly used in furniture that requires frequent movement. On the other hand, Rigid casters have a fixed direction and cannot rotate, providing stability and support. They are ideal for furniture that needs to stay in a fixed position. Both types of casters play a crucial role in the furniture industry by providing mobility and stability for various types of furniture.</p></p>
