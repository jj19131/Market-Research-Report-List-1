<p><p>Electronic Medical Thermometers come in various types to cater to different needs and preferences. Anal thermometers are inserted into the rectum to measure core body temperature. Ear thermometers provide quick and accurate readings by measuring the infrared radiation emitted by the eardrum. Forehead thermometers are non-invasive and ideal for babies and children. Oral thermometers are placed under the tongue to measure temperature. Axillary thermometers are placed in the armpit for a simple and convenient temperature reading. Each type offers unique benefits for monitoring body temperature.</p></p>
