<p><p>Silica matting agents are available in two main types - gel and powder. Gel silica matting agents are typically used for solvent-based systems and provide a higher level of abrasion resistance. On the other hand, powder silica matting agents are more commonly used in water-based systems and offer a smoother finish. Both types of silica matting agents help to reduce gloss levels in coatings and improve the overall appearance of the final product.</p></p>
