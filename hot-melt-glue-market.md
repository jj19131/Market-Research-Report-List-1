<p><p>Hot melt glue market is segmented into different types based on the polymer used. Ethylene Vinyl Acetate has high flexibility and adhesion properties, making it ideal for packaging and woodworking. Polyolefin offers excellent heat resistance and is widely used in automotive and electronics industries. Polyamide provides strong bonding strength and is commonly used in textiles and footwear. Styrenic Block Copolymers offer good thermal stability and are suitable for bookbinding and product assembly. Other types include polyurethane and polyester, offering specific characteristics for various applications.</p></p>
