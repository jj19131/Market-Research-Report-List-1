<p><p>Potentiometer torque sensors are used in various applications to measure the amount of force applied to a rotating element. There are three main types of potentiometer torque sensors: swivel type, gear type, and torsion bar type. </p><p>Swivel type torque sensors use a pivoting mechanism to measure torque, while gear type sensors use gears to transfer force to the potentiometer. Torsion bar sensors measure torque by twisting a metal bar. Each type has its own advantages and specific applications, making them suitable for a range of industries that require accurate torque measurement.</p></p>
