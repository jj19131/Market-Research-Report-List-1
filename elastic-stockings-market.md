<p><p>Elastic stockings are designed to provide compression to the legs, helping to improve blood flow and reduce swelling. Gradient socks are a type of elastic stocking that provides graduated compression, with the highest pressure at the ankle and decreasing pressure moving up the leg. These socks are commonly used to treat varicose veins and edema. Anti-embolism socks are another type of elastic stocking that are specifically designed to help prevent blood clots in the legs, especially for bedridden or immobile patients. Both gradient and anti-embolism socks serve important roles in improving circulation and preventing complications.</p></p>
