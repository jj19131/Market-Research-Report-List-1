<p><p>Coal pollution mitigation solutions refer to various techniques and measures implemented to reduce or minimize the negative environmental impacts associated with the combustion of coal for power generation and industrial processes. Various factors are driving this market growth, including increasing awareness about climate change and environmental degradation, stringent government regulations and policies to limit coal emissions, and growing investments in clean energy sources. Additionally, technological advancements in pollution control equipment and systems are further contributing to market growth.</p><p>The market is witnessing several trends that are shaping its growth. One such trend is the adoption of advanced pollution control technologies, such as flue gas desulfurization, electrostatic precipitators, and fabric filters, which help in reducing sulfur dioxide, particulate matter, and other harmful emissions.
