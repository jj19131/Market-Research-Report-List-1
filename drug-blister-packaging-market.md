<p><p>Drug blister packaging market types include PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), lidding foils, cold form, PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride), and other options. PVC blister packaging offers good barrier properties and cost-effectiveness. Lidding foils are aluminum or paper-based films used as a top lid to seal the blister pack. Cold form packaging provides excellent barrier properties and is tamper-evident. PVDC blister packaging offers high barrier and moisture protection. Other types include high-barrier films like Aclar, polystyrene, and polypropylene. Each type has its benefits and is used based on specific requirements such as barrier protection, cost, and tamper-evidence.</p></p>
