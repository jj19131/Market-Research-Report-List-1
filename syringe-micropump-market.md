<p><strong>The Syringe Micropump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Syringe Pump</li><li>Dual Syringe Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A single syringe pump is a type of syringe micropump that utilizes only one syringe for fluid delivery, making it suitable for simple applications with low flow rates and precise control. On the other hand, a dual syringe pump uses two syringes to deliver fluids, allowing for simultaneous delivery of multiple liquids or different flow rates. This type is preferred for more complex applications requiring higher precision and versatility in fluid delivery. Both types are commonly used in various scientific and medical settings for tasks such as drug delivery, chemical analysis, and microfluidics.</p></p>
