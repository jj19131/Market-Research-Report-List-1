<p><p>The frozen seafood packaging market comprises two main types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging refers to materials that can conform to the shape of the seafood product, such as pouches, bags, or films. It offers advantages like lightweight, cost-effectiveness, and easy storage. Rigid packaging includes materials like trays, containers, or boxes, which provide better protection to the seafood during transportation and storage. Rigid packaging caters to the need for secure and convenient handling of frozen seafood products. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and preservation of frozen seafood.</p></p>
