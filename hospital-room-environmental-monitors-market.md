<p><p>Hospital room environmental monitors are devices used to track various environmental factors in healthcare settings. These monitors can be classified into two types: fixed and portable. Fixed monitors are permanently installed in specific locations within the hospital room, providing continuous monitoring of temperature, humidity, air quality, and other parameters. On the other hand, portable monitors are handheld devices that can be easily moved around the room for spot checks or mobile monitoring. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring a safe and comfortable environment for patients and healthcare staff.</p></p>
