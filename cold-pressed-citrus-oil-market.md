<p><p>Cold pressed citrus oil is a type of essential oil that is extracted from the peels of various citrus fruits including oranges, lemons, grapefruits, and limes. This method helps to preserve the natural aroma, flavor, and therapeutic properties of the oil.</p><p>The global cold pressed citrus oil market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing consumer demand for natural flavors and fragrances. The rising awareness about the health benefits of citrus oils, such as their antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, is also driving market growth. Additionally, the growing use of cold pressed citrus oil in the food and beverage industry as a natural flavoring agent is further propelling market expansion.</p><p>One of the major trends in the cold pressed citrus oil market is the rising popularity of organic and natural products.
