<p><p>Headlamps for passengers come in several types including halogen lamps, xenon lights, and LED headlamps. Halogen lamps are the most common and cost-effective option, providing decent visibility. Xenon lights offer brighter and whiter light than halogen lamps, with longer lifespan. LED headlamps are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and highly durable. Other markets for passenger headlamps include adaptive headlights that adjust to road conditions, laser headlights with longer range, and high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps that provide bright light output. Each type offers different benefits catered to the needs of drivers.</p></p>
