<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Type</li><li>Thermoplastics Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermosetting type and thermoplastics type are two main types of carbon fiber materials used in the automotive industry. Thermosetting type carbon fibers are rigid, durable, and have high heat resistance, making them ideal for structural applications in cars. On the other hand, thermoplastics type carbon fibers are more flexible, lightweight, and easier to mold, making them suitable for components requiring intricate designs. Both types offer unique properties that cater to different automotive requirements, making them valuable materials in the market.</p></p>
