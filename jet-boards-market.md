<p><p>Jet boards are a popular water sport equipment and they can be categorized into two main types: fuel-powered jet boards and battery-powered jet boards. Fuel-powered jet boards are equipped with internal combustion engines that require gasoline or other fuels for operation. These boards are known for their high speed and power but can be noisy and emit exhaust gases. On the other hand, battery-powered jet boards utilize rechargeable batteries to power an electric motor. They offer a quieter and more environmentally friendly alternative, although they may have slightly lower speed and power capabilities compared to fuel-powered boards.</p></p>
