<p><p>Light vehicle ACC radar market can be divided into two main types - OEM and Aftermarket. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) refers to radars that are installed by the vehicle manufacturer during production. These radars are typically integrated into the vehicle's design and may offer advanced features.
