<p><p>Arrhythmia management devices are used to treat irregular heartbeats. The market includes pacemakers, which help regulate heart rhythms by sending electrical impulses to the heart when it beats abnormally. Defibrillators are devices that deliver a shock to the heart to restore normal rhythm in the event of a life-threatening arrhythmia. Ablation devices are used in a minimally invasive procedure to destroy heart tissue that is causing the arrhythmia. These devices play a crucial role in managing and treating various types of arrhythmias.</p></p>
