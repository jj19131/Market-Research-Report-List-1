<p><p>Tailless excavators are commonly divided into two main market types: crawler tailless excavators and wheeled tailless excavators. Crawler tailless excavators are designed with tracks, making them ideal for rough and uneven terrains. They offer better stability and traction compared to wheeled excavators. On the other hand, wheeled tailless excavators are more suitable for smooth and flat surfaces, offering greater mobility and speed. Both types are popular choices in the construction and mining industries for their efficiency and versatility in various job conditions.</p></p>
