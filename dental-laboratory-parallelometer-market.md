<p><p>Dental laboratory parallelometers are tools used to accurately measure and adjust the parallelism of dental prosthetics. There are two main types of parallelometers in the market - 1-arm and 2-arm. </p><p>1-arm parallelometers have a single arm that moves parallel to the measuring surface, making them suitable for measuring single surfaces or angles. On the other hand, 2-arm parallelometers have two arms that move independently, allowing for more complex measurements of multiple surfaces and angles. Both types are essential tools for ensuring the precise fit and functionality of dental prosthetics.</p></p>
