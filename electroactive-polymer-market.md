<p><p>The electroactive polymer market consists of conductive plastics, inherently conductive polymers (ICPs), and inherently dissipative polymers (IDPs). Conductive plastics are polymers with conductive properties, ICPs are polymers that inherently possess electrical conductivity, and IDPs are polymers with static dissipative properties. These types of electroactive polymers are used in various applications such as sensors, actuators, and in the electronics industry. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for different end uses in the market.</p></p>
