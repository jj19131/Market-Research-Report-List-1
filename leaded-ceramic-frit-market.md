<p><p>The Leaded Ceramic Frit market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I frits are typically used in applications where high opacity and hiding power are required, such as in the production of colorful and decorative tiles. On the other hand, Type II frits are preferred for applications that demand high strength and resistance to chemicals and thermal shock, such as in the manufacturing of sanitary ware and kitchenware. Both types cater to specific market needs and offer distinct properties for different end uses.</p></p>
