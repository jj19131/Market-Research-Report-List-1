<p><strong>The Spark Coils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spark coils are essential components in the ignition systems of vehicles. They come in two main types: single-spark and multi-spark coils. Single-spark coils produce one spark per ignition sequence, while multi-spark coils produce multiple sparks per sequence. Single-spark coils are simpler and more common, while multi-spark coils provide a more consistent and efficient ignition process. Both types have their own advantages and are suited for different types of vehicles and operating conditions.</p></p>
