<p><p>Softwood plywood market types are categorized based on thickness into three main categories: below 6mm, 6mm-18mm, and above 18mm. Plywood sheets below 6mm thickness are generally used for applications like packaging, furniture backing, and drawer bottoms due to their lightweight and flexible nature. Sheets between 6mm and 18mm are commonly used for construction purposes like roofing, flooring, and wall sheathing. Plywood sheets above 18mm thickness are typically used for heavy-duty applications such as structural support beams and industrial flooring.</p></p>
