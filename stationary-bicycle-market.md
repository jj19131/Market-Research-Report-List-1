<p><p>Stationary bicycles are divided into two main types within the market: recumbent exercise bikes and upright exercise bikes. Recumbent exercise bikes feature a larger seat with backrest for added comfort during workouts, making them ideal for individuals with back or joint issues. Upright exercise bikes, on the other hand, mimic the feel of a traditional outdoor bicycle with a smaller seat and no backrest, providing a more intense workout experience. Both types offer a convenient and effective way to improve cardiovascular fitness and strength from the comfort of home.</p></p>
