<p><p>Polyamide Resin (PA) market can be segmented into two main types: Reactive Type and Nonreactive Type. Reactive Type polyamide resins are crosslinked with other materials through a chemical reaction, resulting in a durable and heat-resistant coating. On the other hand, Nonreactive Type polyamide resins do not undergo any chemical reaction and are used mainly as binders in inks, adhesives, and coatings. Both types of polyamide resins have their own unique applications and properties, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
