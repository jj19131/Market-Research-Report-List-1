<p><strong>The Turpentine Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fat Turpentine</li><li>Sulphate Turpentine</li><li>Wood Turpentine</li><li>Carbonization Turpentine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turpentine oil market can be classified into four types based on their source and methods of extraction. Fat turpentine is derived from natural resins, while sulphate turpentine is obtained through the Kraft pulping process. Wood turpentine is extracted from coniferous trees, particularly pine, through distillation. Carbonization turpentine is produced during the carbonization of wood. Each type of turpentine oil has unique characteristics and applications in various industries such as paints, varnishes, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
