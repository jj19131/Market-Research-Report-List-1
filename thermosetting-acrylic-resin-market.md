<p><p>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin market includes two main types: Acrylates and Methacrylates. Acrylates are commonly used in surface coatings, adhesives, and elastomers due to their strong adhesion and weather resistance properties. Methacrylates, on the other hand, are preferred for applications requiring optical clarity and excellent resistance to UV radiation. Both types of resins are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics for their durability, versatility, and high-performance characteristics.</p></p>
