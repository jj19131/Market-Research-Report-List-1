<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Coatings</li><li>Solvent Coatings</li><li>Emulsion Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building coatings are products used to protect and enhance the appearance of buildings. Water-soluble coatings are made by dissolving the coating ingredients in water, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent coatings use solvents like mineral spirits to dissolve the coating ingredients, making them durable but less eco-friendly. Emulsion coatings are a type of water-based coating where the resin is suspended in water, offering a good balance between durability and environmental friendliness. Each type of coating has its own advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
