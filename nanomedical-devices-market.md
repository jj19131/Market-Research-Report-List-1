<p><p>Nanomedical devices are small-scale medical devices that utilize nanotechnology for various purposes. Implantable biosensors are nanoscale devices that can be implanted inside the human body to monitor and analyze biological parameters such as glucose levels or blood pressure. These biosensors provide real-time data and can aid in diagnosing and managing various medical conditions. On the other hand, implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) are nanoscale devices used to treat abnormal heart rhythms by delivering electric shocks to restore normal heartbeats. Both these devices play a vital role in improving medical diagnosis and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
