<p><p>Processing Aid (PA) is a type of additive used in polymer processing to improve the flow properties and processability of the polymer. There are two main types of PA markets: Polymer Processing Aid and Acrylic Processing Aid. Polymer Processing Aid helps reduce melt viscosity, increase melt strength, and enhance surface finish in the production of various plastic products. Acrylic Processing Aid, on the other hand, is specifically designed for PVC processing to improve melt strength and elongation properties. Both types of PA play a crucial role in optimizing the manufacturing process of plastic products.</p></p>
