<p><p>Vehicle Event Data Recorders (EDRs) can be classified into two main types: Independent and Integrated. Independent EDRs are standalone devices that can be installed in vehicles to record specific data related to events such as crashes. On the other hand, Integrated EDRs are built into the vehicle's existing systems, such as the airbag control module. This allows for the seamless collection of data without the need for additional hardware. Both types of EDRs play a crucial role in providing valuable information for vehicle safety and accident investigations.</p></p>
